Array#unshift 

    array.unshift(obj, ...)  -> array

    Prepends objects to the front of _array_. other elements up one.

       a = [ "b", "c", "d" ]
       a.unshift("a")   #=> ["a", "b", "c", "d"]
       a.unshift(1, 2)  #=> [ 1, 2, "a", "b", "c", "d"]
